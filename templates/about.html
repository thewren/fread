Sam Finn

Sam Finn taught elementary school for seven years in Washington, D.C., Oakland, and Berkeley. As a school technology lead, he wrote grants, piloted educational software programs, coordinated site technology use, and authored district technology standards. He has also served as a union representative and a dyslexia intervention tutor. Sam holds a dual masters in general and special education and is currently a masters of public policy student at UC Berkeley. He is developing Fread in the spring as his masters' thesis.
